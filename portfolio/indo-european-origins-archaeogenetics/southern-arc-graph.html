About this visualisation

Source paper: Lazaridis, I., et al. (2022). The genetic history of the Southern Arc: A bridge between West Asia and Europe. Science 377. doi:10.1126/science.abm4247 (PMC10064553).

What you're looking at: 77 typed (subject, predicate, object) triples automatically extracted from the paper's full text (abstract, results, discussion), grouped into 104 substantive claims. Each edge is grounded in a specific source sentence; hover any edge to see the claim text. Nodes are coloured by entity category (haplogroup, archaeological culture, geographic region, language branch, ...) drawn from a domain-specific ontology.

Caveats: this is an exploratory automatic extraction, not a review of the paper's conclusions. Hand-spot-check on this paper suggests ~90% triple precision; the remaining ~10% include subject drifts and occasional directional errors (e.g. "Southern Arc located within West Asia" reverses the intended geography). The ontology was LLM-generated from the research question and patched once to add haplogroup->region and haplogroup->time signatures the initial draft lacked.

Pipeline: produced by Evidensity's meta-analysis pipeline.
